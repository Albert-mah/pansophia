现在进行时 Present Continuous

现在进行时表示此刻正在发生的动作，或这段时间内持续进行的事情。看到 Look! / Listen! / now / at the moment，就用它。

🔑 一句话抓住它

现在进行时 = am / is / are（be 动词）+ 动词 -ing，表示此刻正在做某事。

例：I am eating.   She is running.   They are playing.

1. am / is / are 怎么配主语

主语用哪个 be例句
IamI am reading a book.
YouareYou are watching TV.
He / She / ItisHe is sleeping. / It is raining.
We / TheyareWe are having lunch.

2. 三种句型：肯定 / 否定 / 疑问

句型结构例句
✅ 肯定句主语 + am/is/are + 动词ingShe is dancing now.
❌ 否定句主语 + am/is/are + not + 动词ingShe is not dancing. / She isn't dancing.
❓ 一般疑问句Am/Is/Are + 主语 + 动词ing…?Is she dancing? — Yes, she is. / No, she isn't.
✅ 疑问句回答口诀

问 Is he…? → 答 Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.
问 Are they…? → 答 Yes, they are. / No, they aren't.
问 Am I…? → 答 Yes, you are. / No, you aren't.（注意人称变换）

3. 动词 -ing 怎么加（拼写规则）

情况变法例子
一般情况直接 + ingplay → playing，read → reading，eat → eating
以不发音的 e 结尾去掉 e，再 + ingmake → making，write → writing，dance → dancing，live → living
重读闭音节（辅元辅）双写末尾辅音，+ ingrun → running，sit → sitting，swim → swimming，get → getting，put → putting
以 ie 结尾ie → y，+ ingdie → dying，lie → lying，tie → tying
⚠️ 最容易出错的 3 个地方

1. 动词 -ing 之前 be 动词不能省：She dancing. ✗ → She is dancing. ✓

2. 重读闭音节要双写：run → running（不是 runing），sit → sitting（不是 siting）。

3. 状态动词一般不用进行时：know / like / love / hate / want / need / understand → 用一般现在时。
  ✗ I am knowing.   ✓ I know.

4. 时间标志词（信号词）

看到这些词，想到现在进行时：

now   right now   at the moment   Look!   Listen!   at present

另外，如果说"这段时间（虽然不是此刻）在做某事"也用进行时：
I am learning English this year.（这一年都在学英语）

✏️ 例句对照

1. Look! The dog is running in the garden.
   看！那条狗正在花园里跑。

2. Listen! Someone is singing upstairs.
   听！有人在楼上唱歌。

3. We are having a meeting now.
   我们现在正在开会。（注意：have→having，去掉不发音的 e）
